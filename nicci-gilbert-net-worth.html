What is Nicci Gilbert's Net Worth?

Nicci Gilbert is an American singer, producer, and entrepreneur, who has a net worth of $10 million dollars. However, internal strife would slowly drive the group apart, and all of the various members have gone on to other endeavors. Nicci Gilbert runs her own artist development business called, N-Go Entertainment, and she also produces theater, film, and television projects. She pursued a solo career for a time, and is currently working on a new Brownstone album with Charmayne Maxwell and new group member, Teisha Brown. She is also currently appearing on the hit TV One reality series, "R&B Divas".
