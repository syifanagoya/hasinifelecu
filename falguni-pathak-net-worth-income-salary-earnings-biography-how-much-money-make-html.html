Falguni Pathak (Gujarati: , Hindi: ) (born March 12, 1971) is an Indian singer and performing artist who is located in Mumbai. Falguni Pathak is a member of the Falguni Pathak vocal group. A traditional musical style from the Indian state of Gujarat serves as the foundation for her music. Since making her professional debut in 1998, she has grown into an artist with a huge following throughout the country of India. In response to a question about how she came to choose singing as a profession, she said that it was a matter of chance. It was in 1998 that she launched her first album, and she has also recorded a large number of songs for Bollywood films. The subject of love runs across the majority of her songs. 

She has been in many performances across India and other countries, accompanied by a band known as Tha Thaiyaa. A number of television programmes, including Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah, Kaun Banega Crorepati, Star Dandiya Dhoom, and Comedy Nights With Kapil, have featured her in guest roles. She has a number of very famous songs to her credit, which are still heard and loved across India. Her albums are not only well-known for their catchy tunes, but also for the sweet love tales that are portrayed in the songs. 

She is particularly well-liked within the Gujarati population, and she is often invited to play at major events such as Navratri. A few of her most famous songs include “Chudi jo khanki haath ma,” “Maine payal hai chhankai,” “Meri Chunar Udd Udjaye,” and “Ayi Pardesh se Pariyon ki Rani.” She also has a number of other songs that are well-known.
