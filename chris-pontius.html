Chris Pontius

Chris Pontius nicknamed Party Boy, an American stunt performer and actor, is best known for his role as one of the cast members of the MTV stunt show Jackass. He’s also the co-host of Wildboyz, a Jackass spinoff. For his pranks on Wildboyz, he gained notoriety for wearing very little clothing, either a headband and belt or only a male pouch thong and a bow tie.

Chris Pontius’ Childhood and Education

Chris Pontius was born Christopher Andrew Pontius under the sign of Cancer on July 16, 1974 in Pasadena, California. Pontius is of White ancestry and is of American nationality. Pontius went to high school in San Luis Obispo.

Career TV Series of Chris Pontius

Chris Pontius made his television debut in 2000 on the MTV reality series Jackass, which starred Johnny Knoxville, Steve-O, Bam Margera, and Ryan Dunn among eleven stuntmen. From 2000 to 2002, Pontius was a regular on the show, appearing in 21 episodes.

Pontius and Steve-O co-hosted the MTV2 Jackass spin-off series Wildboyz in 2003. Totally Busted, Jackassworld.com: 24 Hour Takeover, and Loiter Squad are among his other works.

Feature films

Pontius made his big screen debut in Jeff Tremaine’s film Jackass: The Movie, which was released in 2002. In 2007, he starred alongside Steve-O, Preston Lacy, and Jason Acun in the comedy film National Lampoon’s TV: The Movie as Jesus Christ.

As of 2018, he is starring in Tim Kirkby’s action comedy flick Action Point.
