What is Michael Mitnick's Net Worth?

Michael Mitnick is an American film and television writer, director, songwriter and composer who has a net worth of $6 million. Michael Mitnick served as executive story editor and writer for the television series Vinyl in 2016. He wrote for the movie The Giver in 2014 and The Current War in 2017. Mitnick was a composer for the short Well Fed and Comfortable in 2006.

He also served as executive producer for the 2017 film The Current War and wrote the song "Christmas You Go So Fast" for the TV series Vinyl in 2016. He was ranked #7 on the Black List of Best Unproduced Screenplays in 2011. Mitnick has also worked on plays and has been nominated for a Drama Desk Award for Outstanding Musical and a Drama Desk Award for Outstanding Book of a Musical.
