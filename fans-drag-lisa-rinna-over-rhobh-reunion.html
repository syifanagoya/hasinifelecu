Fans Drag Lisa Rinna Over 'RHOBH' Reunion

Lisa Rinna may have pushed fans over the edge after the final installment of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills reunion.

A lot of the drama focused on her and Kathy Hilton, since Lisa insisted that she was owed an apology from Kathy after Kathy’s blow-up on their Aspen trip. Although the issue really was between Kathy and her sister, Kyle Richards, Lisa wouldn’t back down.

Now, fans wonder why Lisa was allowed to call the situation “abuse,” when her other costars had to defend their own feelings.
