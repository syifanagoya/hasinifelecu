Early Life

Middleton was born to parents Michael and Carole Middleton on January 9, 1982, in Berkshire County of England. Both her parents used to work in an airline company. She had her fair share of conflicts in early school life. Middleton had to leave Downe House girls school due to bullying from seniors. Middleton graduated from the University of St. Andrews in Fife, Scotland, in the arts field.
